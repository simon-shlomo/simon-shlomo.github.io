Our services

Detailed quantifications of drug-induced neuro-physiological changes in brain activity.

Our services are based on the latest developments in machine-learning and big data analytics. Using an advanced data pipeline we pre-process and clean EEG data, comprehensively map biomarkers in the spectral, temporal and spatial domain, and we identify highly sensitive indices unique to specific drug interventions.

EEG biomarkers

Secondary endpoints for clinical trials

The EEG measures brain activity with a high temporal resolution and is particularly sensitive to alterations in the excitatory/inhibitory balance, which is compromised in several neuropsychiatric disorders1.

We have developed novel biomarkers that are sensitive to modulations of the excitatory/inhibitory balance (patients, model and hippocampal slices)2,3,4. These biomarkers play an important role in understanding drug effects in humans and therefor have great potential as secondary endpoints in clinical trials.

Through our EEG analysis pipeline we comprehensively map and select neurophysiological biomarkers in the spectral, temporal, and spatial domain to quantify changes in continuous EEG recordings.

Drug indices

Beyond single biomarkers

The EEG can be parameterized in multiple biomarkers, which complicates the assessment of drug-induced effects and leads to reduced statistical power for...

We have developed an EEG analysis pipeline which identifies the right combination of multiple biomarkers and integrates the data into an index highly sensitive to a specific intervention or patient group5.

The integrated index can for example be used for prognostic purposes or provide a single value quantification of drug effects for dosage studies. By combining multiple biomarkers into a single index we achieve a higher statistical power compared to a single biomarker based analysis.

About us

“We strive to improve and accelerate CNS drug development through innovative EEG analysis.”

NBT Analytics B.V. is a Dutch spin-off company from the VU University Amsterdam. The company is founded in August 2015 by Dr. Klaus Linkenkaer-Hansen, Dr. Simon-Shlomo Poil, and Medrithm Holding B.V. The company is funded by VU University Amsterdam and Medrithm Holding B.V.

NBT Analytics is build on the Neurophysiological Biomarker Toolbox (NBT), a research platform for M/EEG analysis developed at the VU University Amsterdam.
